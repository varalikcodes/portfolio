Company Overview

BestLogic Staffing is a recruitment and staffing company that connects talented professionals with organizations across various industries. They specialize in finding the right fit between candidates and companies, emphasizing both technical skills and cultural alignment.

Target Audience & Demographics

Primary Audience: HR professionals, business owners, and job seekers

Demographics: Ages 25-55, corporate professionals, small to medium business owners

Pain Points: Time-consuming hiring processes, difficulty finding qualified candidates, need for trustworthy recruitment partners

Goals: Streamline hiring, find quality talent quickly, build long-term recruitment relationships

Design Strategy & Thought Process

For BestLogic Staffing, I focused on creating designs that convey professionalism, trustworthiness, and efficiency. The color palette emphasizes corporate blues and greens to inspire confidence, while maintaining approachable elements to appeal to both employers and job seekers.

Key Design Principles:

  • Professional yet approachable aesthetic
  • Clear, readable typography for business communications
  • Consistent branding across all materials
  • Modern layouts that reflect industry innovation

Company Overview

FreeAxez is an innovative AI and enterprise solutions company focused on automating business processes and improving operational efficiency through intelligent technology solutions. They specialize in data-driven automation tools and AI-powered business intelligence.

Target Audience & Demographics

Primary Audience: Enterprise decision-makers, IT professionals, business operations managers

Pain Points: Manual processes, data silos, inefficient workflows, need for scalable solutions

For FreeAxez, I emphasized a cutting-edge, tech-forward design approach that communicates innovation and reliability. Key Design Principles:

  • Data visualization elements to represent AI/analytics
  • Professional color scheme (blues, grays, accent colors)
  • Scalable designs for various digital platforms

Company Overview

Puratos is a global leader in bakery, patisserie, and chocolate ingredients, with a strong focus on food innovation and R&D. They develop cutting-edge solutions for food manufacturers and artisans, emphasizing sustainability and quality in the food industry.

Target Audience & Demographics

Primary Audience: R&D teams, food scientists, business development professionals, strategic decision-makers

Demographics: Ages 25-55, scientific and technical professionals, global food industry stakeholders

Pain Points: Complex partnership decisions, need for data-driven R&D choices, streamlining innovation processes

Goals: Make informed R&D partnership decisions, accelerate innovation, optimize resource allocation

Design Strategy & Thought Process

For Puratos, I focused on creating a clean, scientific aesthetic that communicates precision and innovation. The design approach emphasized clarity and functionality, using a structured layout that mirrors scientific methodology while maintaining visual appeal for business stakeholders.

Key Design Principles:

  • Clean, scientific interface design prioritizing usability
  • Structured information hierarchy for complex decision-making
  • Professional color palette reflecting food industry standards
  • Visual representations of decision trees and data flows
